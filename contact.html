Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our manufacturing process

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type and complexity. Generally, we require a minimum of 500 pieces per design for standard products, but we can accommodate smaller orders for established clients or unique projects. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we have a dedicated design team that can help bring your ideas to life. We offer complete custom design services from concept development, technical drawings, pattern making, sample development, to final production. Our designers work closely with you to ensure your vision is perfectly executed.

What is your typical production timeline?

Production timelines depend on order quantity and complexity. Typically, samples take 7-10 days for development, and bulk production takes 4-6 weeks after sample approval. We provide detailed production schedules for each project and maintain regular communication throughout the process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we have extensive experience shipping to clients in Europe, North America, Middle East, and Asia. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and logistics. Our shipping partners ensure reliable and timely delivery to your specified location.

What quality control measures do you have?

We implement rigorous quality control at every stage: fabric inspection, in-process quality checks during cutting and sewing, and final inspection before shipping. We're ISO 9001 certified and follow international quality standards to ensure every product meets your expectations.

Can you work with our designs and specifications?

Absolutely! We specialize in working with client designs and specifications. You can provide tech packs, samples, or even just design concepts. Our team will work with you to ensure accurate translation of your designs into high-quality manufactured products.
