Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type and complexity. Generally, we require a minimum of 500 pieces per design, but we can accommodate smaller orders for established clients or sample development.

Do you offer custom design services?

Yes, we have a dedicated design team that can help bring your ideas to life. We offer complete custom design services from concept to final product, including pattern making and sample development.

What is your production timeline?

Production timelines depend on order quantity and complexity. Typically, samples take 7-10 days, and bulk production takes 4-6 weeks after sample approval. We provide detailed timelines for each project.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we have extensive experience shipping to clients in Europe, North America, Middle East, and Asia. We handle all export documentation, customs clearance, and logistics for seamless international delivery.

What quality certifications do you have?

We are certified with ISO 9001:2015 for quality management, OEKO-TEX Standard 100 for textile safety, BSCI for social compliance, and GOTS for organic textiles.

Can you work with our existing designs?

Absolutely! We can work with your existing designs, patterns, and specifications. Our technical team will review your requirements and provide manufacturing solutions that meet your quality standards.
